
## Lessons learned

1. **Format contracts must be enforced at emitter level.** When a tool's contract is established, ensure all emitters follow it.
2. **Dry-run testing of new bridges is not enough.** Real consumer integration test is needed.
3. **Silent failure modes are dangerous.** `dedup_gate` should probably WARN when it skips entries due to missing fields, not silently continue.
