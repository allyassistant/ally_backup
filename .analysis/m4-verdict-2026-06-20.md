## Why STAY SHADOW (not ACTIVATE)

Per #162 M4 spec, ACTIVATE requires:
- ✅ validSamples ≥ 100 (currently 0)
- ✅ bothJudgeSuccess ≥ 95% (currently no data)
- ✅ splitRate ≤ 50% (currently no data)
