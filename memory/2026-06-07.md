## 15:47 — Cron Model Resolution: Devil's Advocate Challenge 🚨

### Blindspots Sub-Agent Finding
Devil's Advocate sub-agent (MiniMax-M3) directly read session `.jsonl` files and concluded **our entire premise was wrong**.

- Auto runs showing `model: "deepseek-v4-flash"` may have **started with MiniMax** and fallen back

- The "auto vs manual" discrepancy may simply be **MiniMax provider instability timing**

### 3 Hypotheses Challenged
| Hypothesis | Confidence After Challenge |
