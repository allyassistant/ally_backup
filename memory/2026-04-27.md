### v2.4.2 新功能：Effective Carat Weight

**公式：** `Effective Carat = Actual Carat × Spread Ratio`

**輸出範例：**
```
📉 Effective Carat: 10.04ct → 8.03ct
  10.04ct 實際重量，因 Spread 80% 僅視覺相當於 8.03ct
```

**應用場景：**
- Spread < 100% = 視覺偏輕 (📉)
- Spread > 100% = 視覺偏大 (📈)
